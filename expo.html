Expo 2030 – Riyadh, Saudi Arabia

Expo 2030 is a global platform where countries, organizations, and innovators come together to present solutions, build partnerships, and share knowledge that shapes the future. Riyadh’s selection as the host marks a historic milestone for Saudi Arabia and the Middle East.

Why Riyadh?

With its rapidly growing economy, modern infrastructure, and cultural heritage, Riyadh is at the forefront of transformation. The city is undergoing massive development to align with Vision 2030, which aims to diversify the Kingdom's economy and promote sustainable growth.

Vision 2030 and Expo 2030

Vision 2030 is Saudi Arabia’s long-term plan to reduce dependence on oil, invest in public services, and position the Kingdom as a hub for innovation and tourism. Expo 2030 will act as a catalyst for these goals by:

  • Showcasing Saudi innovation, entrepreneurship, and youth potential.
  • Encouraging global partnerships in technology and sustainability.
  • Creating long-term economic opportunities and jobs.
  • Enhancing the global perception of Saudi Arabia as an open, collaborative nation.

Expo 2030 Themes

  • Innovation for Humanity – Harnessing science and technology for the common good.
  • Global Sustainability – Building greener cities and smarter ecosystems.
  • Connectivity and Mobility – Exploring the future of transport, communication, and digital transformation.
  • Cultural Exchange – Celebrating diversity and unity among nations.

Benefits to the World

Expo 2030 Riyadh will welcome millions of visitors from all over the world. It will serve as a space for collaboration on climate change, artificial intelligence, smart cities, water security, and energy efficiency. The event is expected to leave a lasting legacy for future generations.

Shaping the Future Together

Riyadh invites the world to be part of a transformative journey. Expo 2030 is more than an exhibition—it's a global conversation about how we can build a better world together. Through creativity, technology, and shared purpose, Riyadh 2030 will inspire progress that transcends borders.
